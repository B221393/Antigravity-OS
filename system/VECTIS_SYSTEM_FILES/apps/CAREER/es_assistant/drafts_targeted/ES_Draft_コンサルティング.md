# コンサルティング 向けESドラフト

## Title:  breakthrough

## Explanation

This PR draft focuses on demonstrating the applicant's ability to think strategically, solve problems, and inspire others. The piece emphasizes their ability to leverage their background in consulting to identify and address challenges faced by professionals in their field.

**Keywords used:**

* **EGO**: Referencing the user's identity core, which represents their values, beliefs, and experiences.
* **Legacy**: Symbolic of the impact they want to leave on the field.
* **Collaboration**: Highlighting their ability to work effectively in teams.
* **Innovation**: Reflecting their ability to develop new and groundbreaking solutions.

**Structure:**

* Introduction:
  * Starts with the user's identity core.
  * Explains the importance of their background and how it contributes to their success.
* Body:
  * Presents their consulting experience and how it led to successful problem-solving.
  * Emphasizes their ability to think strategically and inspire others to achieve their goals.
  * Highlights their collaborative and innovative approach to problem-solving.
* Conclusion:
  * Summarizes their qualifications and expresses their confidence in their ability to make a significant impact on the field.

**Overall, this PR draft showcases the applicant's impressive background, skills, and values that make them a valuable asset to any organization.**
